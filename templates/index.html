What is Medical Insurance?

Medical insurance is a contract that requires your health insurer to pay some or all of your healthcare costs in exchange for a premium. It helps protect you and your family from the high costs of medical care, including hospital stays, surgeries, doctor visits, prescription drugs, preventive care, and more.

Key benefits of medical insurance:

  • Reduces your out-of-pocket expenses for medical care
  • Provides access to a wide network of healthcare providers
  • Covers preventive services like vaccinations and screenings
  • Offers financial protection in case of serious illness or accident
  • May include additional services such as mental health support, maternity care, and wellness programs

Medical insurance can be obtained through employers, government programs, or purchased individually. Policies vary in terms of coverage, premiums, deductibles, and co-payments. Having medical insurance gives you peace of mind and helps ensure you get the care you need when you need it.
